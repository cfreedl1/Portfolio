Clean Machine

The Clean Machine is a local car wash. I revamped their Facebook page, kept their website updated, and created multiple ad campaigns for them. The highlights are shown below.

Advertisements

Holiday Season

  • Goal: Promote sale of gift cards
  • Length: Novermber 30 - December 23, 2020
  • Results: Reached 4,712 people and received 116 link clicks ($0.99 CPC).

Washify App

  • Goal: Gain awareness and promote the Washify App.
  • Length: May 18 - June 1, 2020
  • Results: Reached 2,743 people and received 52 link clicks ($0.84 CPC). Between POP displays and online advertising, 70 people have downloaded the app so far.

Father's Day

  • Goal: Gain awareness and promote the sale of gift cards
  • Length: June 15 - June 22, 2020
  • Results: Reached 5,440 people and received 33 link clicks ($1.26 CPC).

Social Media Posts

Overall Results

  • Website: Increased user activity from ~0 to between 64-117 users a day.
    Added events to website to create greater understanding of user experience.
  • Facebook Page: Increased page likes from 20 to 157 likes.
    Organic posts reached approximately 3,000 people per a month on average.
    Created a consistent flow of organic content.
